Course Overview

Introduction to linear equations, inequalities, and systems of equations. Students will learn to solve and graph these equations and inequalities.
Exploration of geometric shapes, theorems, and proofs. Topics include angles, triangles, the Pythagorean theorem, and the basics of transformations.
Data Analysis and Probability: Understanding and interpreting data through mean, median, mode, and range. Students will also learn about probability concepts and simple experiments.
Strengthening arithmetic skills with integers, fractions, decimals, and rational numbers. Emphasis on operations, order of operations, and number properties.
Introduction to the concept of functions and their representations. Students will learn about linear functions and their applications.
