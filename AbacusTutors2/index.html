Abacus Tutors offers academic services with the mission to foster academic growth and development of their students in a supportive environment. The goal of tutoring is to help students overcome academic challenges and promote academic independence. Tutoring provides one-on-one attention and individualized explanation to help students learn how to learn and become autonomous learners. The focus is on overcoming academic challenges while promoting academic independence, avoiding dependence on the tutor.
